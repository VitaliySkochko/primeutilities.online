1. Categories of Personal Data We Collect

  • Data you provide: gender, age category, email, feedback, and other info you voluntarily enter.
  • Data collected automatically: IP address, device type and model, OS version, time zone, language settings, hardware ID, advertising ID (IDFA/GAID), usage patterns, cookies.
  • Data from third parties: analytics and marketing partners may provide data on how you found our app or website.

2. Purposes of Processing

We process your personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Analyze usage and improve performance (using Google Analytics, Firebase, Amplitude, Appsflyer, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Serve and measure ads, including personalized advertising.
  • Communicate with you (support, notifications, offers).
  • Process payments for premium features or subscriptions.
  • Enforce our Terms and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases (EEA Users)

For users in the EEA, we process personal data under the following bases:

  • Contract performance (providing the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (analyzing usage, sending marketing messages, improving features).
  • Legal obligations (tax, law enforcement).

4. Sharing of Personal Data

We share your data with trusted third-party providers:

  • Cloud hosting (Google Firebase).
  • Analytics (Google, Appsflyer, Amplitude, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Marketing partners (ad networks, email delivery).
  • Payment processors.
  • Law enforcement or regulators, when legally required.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to processing.

6. Age Limitation

We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 16. If you learn that a child has provided data, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored outside your country of residence. Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and other safeguards.

8. Advertising and Tracking Technologies

Our app may request permission to use your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) or other identifiers to measure the performance of marketing campaigns and improve user experience.

This data is used only for:

  • Measuring app installs, launches, and engagement events.
  • Understanding the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
  • Delivering personalized offers and in-app experiences.

We do not use tracking data to identify you personally, and we do not sell or share this information with third parties for unrelated marketing purposes.

If you choose to deny tracking permission, the app will continue to function normally.
You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device settings:
Settings → Privacy → Tracking

9. Analytics

We use third-party analytics services (such as AppsFlyer, RevenueCat, Amplitude) to help us understand how users interact with our app and to improve performance and stability.

These tools may collect anonymized information such as:

  • Device type, operating system, and version.
  • App usage data and event logs.
  • Non-identifiable device identifiers (IDFV).

All analytics data is handled in compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) policy and GDPR/CCPA regulations.

When you launch the app, you may see a prompt asking to allow tracking. By granting permission, you consent to limited data collection described above for analytics and performance purposes. You can withdraw your consent anytime via your device settings.

16. Data Protection and Security

Prime Cleaner does not collect, store, or share any sensitive personal data, such as biometric identifiers, payment card details, health information, or precise location data. All information processed by the app (e.g., anonymous analytics events, device model, or advertising identifier) is strictly non-sensitive and used only to improve app performance and provide essential functionality.

To ensure data security, we apply the following measures:

Encryption in transit and at rest: All transmitted data is protected using HTTPS/TLS, and stored data is encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent standards.

Access limitation: Only authorized personnel can access system data, and all such access is logged and monitored.

No sale or transfer: We do not sell, rent, or disclose any user information to third parties for marketing or unrelated purposes.

Secure cloud storage: Any limited data stored in Google Firebase or RevenueCat is anonymized and protected according to their respective security and privacy certifications (GDPR, CCPA, ISO/IEC 27001).

Minimal retention: Data is kept only for the duration necessary to provide the Service and is permanently deleted or anonymized afterward.

Prime Cleaner fully complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, GDPR, and CCPA requirements for data minimization and protection.
