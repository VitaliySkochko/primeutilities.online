1. Categories of Personal Data We Collect

  • Data you provide: gender, age category, email, feedback, and other info you voluntarily enter.
  • Data collected automatically: IP address, device type and model, OS version, time zone, language settings, hardware ID, advertising ID (IDFA/GAID), usage patterns, cookies.
  • Data from third parties: analytics and marketing partners may provide data on how you found our app or website.

2. Purposes of Processing

We process your personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Analyze usage and improve performance (using Google Analytics, Firebase, Amplitude, Appsflyer, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Serve and measure ads, including personalized advertising.
  • Communicate with you (support, notifications, offers).
  • Process payments for premium features or subscriptions.
  • Enforce our Terms and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases (EEA Users)

For users in the EEA, we process personal data under the following bases:

  • Contract performance (providing the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (analyzing usage, sending marketing messages, improving features).
  • Legal obligations (tax, law enforcement).

4. Sharing of Personal Data

We share your data with trusted third-party providers:

  • Cloud hosting (Google Firebase).
  • Analytics (Google, Appsflyer, Amplitude, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Marketing partners (ad networks, email delivery).
  • Payment processors.
  • Law enforcement or regulators, when legally required.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to processing.

6. Age Limitation

We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 16. If you learn that a child has provided data, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored outside your country of residence. Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and other safeguards.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.

California residents have the right to request information about personal data shared with third parties for direct marketing. To exercise this right, please contact us.

10. Data Retention
